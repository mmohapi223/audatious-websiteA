About

Audacious Leaders Network is a youth-led non-profit making organization of Basotho organizing for change and development. It is committed to empowering and equipping young women and youth with social entrepreneurship, leadership trainings, community engagement and dialogues.

Vision

We envision a world where youth and women are co-creators, co-leaders and advocates in Lesotho’s development.

Mission

We equip and empower young women and youth to lead, organize and advocate for Sustainable Development in their communities.

Organizational Values

Integrity: We are guided by our strong ethical principles whenever we work in our communities to always safeguard the lives of those we serve.

Innovation: We continuously strive to find groundbreaking, disruptive ways to solve the challenges facing youths and young women.

Courage: We stand up for the rights of others, and for speak up in the face of injustice. We are brave in the pursuit of initiatives for the social good.

Collaboration: We believe in working together with our partners to achieve social impact within the communities we work in.
